Dry Oar Site Plan

Sherri Walter

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to market the Dry Oar Whitewater Rafting company. It will do so by providing detailed information such as schedule, pricing, waivers, and what to expect on your adventure. It will also provide information about the experience of the staff in taking out groups for rafting.

Audience

The ideal customer for Dry Oar are athletic families, individuals and groups looking for an outdoor adventure that will bring them closer to nature and each other. Customer looking for a growth experience and enjoy being challenged will want experienced guides and safe equipment.
