Seasonal Gardening

by Susan Royall

Indoors or outdoors, there is always something to be done for the garden. Composting year round can provide a great resource for excellence soil come spring. Current recommendations for indoor planting in March are:

  • Bell Peppers
  • Celery
  • Eggplants

April recommendations for indoor planting are:

  • Broccoli
  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Cabbage
  • Cauliflower
  • Lettuce
  • Swiss Chard
  • Tomatoes

Outdoor planting is not recommended at this time.
