Blooming Report

Spring means green and we are not seeing much of it yet. A few shoot and sprouts are beginning to poke up. As weather warms, we can add to our garden, but will want to protect from freezing. Consider wrapping or cloches to give early spring planting a chance to thrive. Time for hearty plantings, and protections to be in place. Watch overwatering, and heed recommendations for location and sun. Reputable garden centers will provide recommendations for your area. Your cooperation extension service is also a good resource. Consider starting plants indoors to be brought out when weather warms. Wide gardeners will take caution and use prudence when beginning their gardens. Check here for weather updates!
