Have you ever felt completely alone? That is how I felt when I found out my declining health is caused by pectus excavatum, a chest wall deformity. There are not as many women as men who have it and most who chronicle their journey are younger males. I longed to find stories of women like me who need surgery. I can provide personal experience, information, encouragement and hope through articles and video log of my experience. My site, Dear Candy Dish will chronicle my experience and journey.

Dear Candy Dish is for anyone with pectus excavatum looking for information and hope.
