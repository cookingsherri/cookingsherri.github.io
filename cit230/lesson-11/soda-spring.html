Musical Tradition

by Carole King

Bring your instrument and jam out with other artists at our Geyser Song Day. This year there will be concessions, craft vendors, and activities all day. Musician and artisans share their artistry in celebration of our annual geyser celebration. Enjoy a walk in the park, grab a bite to eat and then shop to sounds of our local talent.

Visits to our Fish Haven Geyser are welcome. Our visitor's center has information on suggested times and ticket prices. Sturdy comfortable shoes are recommended. Knowlegeable guides are available to answer questions
